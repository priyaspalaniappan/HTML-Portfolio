The best movies According to Priya

My most fav movies of all-time

Iruthi Suttru

The film follows the story of a boxing coach who discovers a talented young female boxer and trains her to become a champion

Vanakkam Chennai

After being cheated by Narayanan, a house agent, Ajay and Anjali end up sharing the same apartment. Love blossoms between the two while they attempt to catch Narayanan.

Kannum Kannum Kollaiyadithaal

Two fraudsters involved in online shopping scams fall in love and eventually decide to give up their shady business, oblivious to the real intentions of the women they love.
